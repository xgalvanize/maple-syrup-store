# Maple Syrup Product Images

Add your maple syrup product images here:

- `light-maple.jpg` - Light/Golden maple syrup bottle (500ml)
- `dark-maple.jpg` - Dark maple syrup bottle (500ml)

Image requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Size: 400x400px or larger (will be scaled)
- Content: Product photo of bottle/container

The frontend will serve these images from `/images/` path.
